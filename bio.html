Biography

Sundar Pichai or Sundararajan Pichai is an Indian born American who was CEO of Google(2015-) and its parenting Company Alphabet(2019-)

Sundar Pichai was born in an poor middle class family in Madras(Now Chennai, Tamil Nadu), India. While growing up he used to sleep with his brother in living room of cramped family room. Pichai's father was working at itish International GEC. His father saw that he needs a good education for the better future. Pichai had good memory power with the telephone numbers. Once he knew the telephone numbers he would never forget it. Pichai from the very young age showed interest in Technology. His father was aware that Sundar should get good education so when he was supposed to go America for his studies at Stanford though his father couldnt afford the flight for America yet he went and completed his Studies

According to Pichai 3 things had changed his life a fridge, TV and Telephone. As mentioned earlier Pichai was interested in Technology. He noticed that when the telephone the first device which came to his home made his father's work easier. Before that his father used to go to phone booth for calling it used to take 30 mins to go to the booth, 30 mins to wait for the turn and more 30 mins to come back home but when the telephone came in his house he could do it in some mins or seconds. From the incident Sundar knew that technology saves time and make the work easier.

After his great education and coding skills he landed with job in Google. He was a problem solver. When he landed with job in google those days Microsoft had the top browser in the world Internet Explorer.
